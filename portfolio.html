Advanced Healthcare

For my final project I had the opportunity to work with Advanced Healthcare a small chiropractor in Fort Collins, Colorado. Their main priorities are to add an online scheduler to help streamline scheduling customers appointments and an online shop.

Working with Advanced Healthcare taught me how to better work with non-IT customers, cater to my partners needs, and of course programming. As it pertains to programming I learned about connecting the Google Calendar API with an active business. I found out that even though I could make an intricate interactive scheduler online, making something and getting people to use it is two entirely different things. When I brought my initial ideas to Doctor Irwin he was impressed with the power of computers, but he was skeptical about his employees learning and correctly using the technology. Doctor Irwin , without even knowing it, showed me there has to be a balance between how intricate the software is and its simplicity of use.

PiOS

PiOS was definetly the hardest project I have worked on. This was a team project and we decided we wanted to create a completely secure data streaming/transfer app and we wanted to connect it to a local Raspberry Pi as our server. By not connecting to the internet this greatly increased our secruity right off the bat. Our next step was to create an iphone app where we could authenicate our users and allow them to connect to our servers. The fun part was I knew nothing about Linux or Raspberry Pis, I had never created an Iphone App, or code my own authenication and authorization system.
