For my final project I got to work with Advanced Healthcare a small chiropractor in Fort Collins, Colorado. Their main priorities are to add an online scheduler to help streamline scheduling customers appointments and an online shop.

Working with Advanced Healthcare taught me how to better work with non-IT customers, cater to my partners needs, and of course programming. As it pertains to programming I learned about connecting the Google Calendar API with an active business. I found out that even though I could make an intricate interactive scheduler online, making something and getting people to use it is two entirely different things. When I brought my initial ideas to Doctor Irwin he was impressed with the power of computers, but he was skeptical about his employees learning and correctly using the technology. Doctor Irwin , without even knowing it, showed me there has to be a balance between how intricate the software is and its simplicity of use.
